Pipeline

How It Works

A complete end-to-end data pipeline — from raw upload to actionable intelligence — in four simple steps.

1

Upload Your Raw Data

Drop in a CSV, Excel sheet, or link your SQL database. No complex data modeling or coding required.

2

Instant Cleaning & Scoring

The engine automatically fixes duplicates, fills missing values, and assigns your dataset an official Data Health Score.

3

Auto-Generated Dashboards

The AI instantly builds interactive charts and industry-specific KPIs customized precisely to your business type.

4

Consult the Decision Center

Review AI-generated action plans with confidence scores and expected business impacts, ready for managers to approve.
